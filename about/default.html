About Us


FinanceScript is a financial website that provides information and calculation tools for personal and corporate finance. Since July 2015, the website has continued to add features that facilitate valuations for fixed incomes, equities, derivatives, alternative investments and portfolios. This success has been possible thanks to dedication to achieve mission-driven goals, and professionals who kindly provided invaluable advice and information.


Understanding the ever-complex financial world is rewarding and demanding. FinanceScript's mission is to (1) help people that lack finance background to become more financially literate, and (2) save professional's time by doing calculations and providing up-to-date news. FinanceScript attempts to provide accurate information by using reliable resources, and hopes to bring the society one step closer to equal information distribution. Lastly, it is important to mention that some professionals, friends, and colleagues contributed to make this website more relevant. FinanceScript expresses huge gratitude toward them.
